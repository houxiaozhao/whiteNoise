About White Noise and Binaural Beats

White noise is a type of sound that contains all frequencies distributed evenly, effectively masking disturbing environmental noises and helping people sleep better and relax. Binaural beats are an advanced sound wave technology that creates specific frequency beats in the brain by delivering pure tones with slightly different frequencies to each ear, helping to regulate brainwaves and promote deep relaxation and improved sleep quality. On our platform, you can find high-quality white noise, binaural beats, blue noise, and brown noise, all carefully modulated to create an ideal environment for sleep.
